## 1. Advanced Global Targeting (International SEO)
Our approach to global growth is rooted in signaling multi-regional capability. We don't just "export" English content; we architect for local relevance.

### Hreflang Implementation
By implementing `hreflang` tags, we tell search engines exactly which regional version of a page to show. Whether a client is searching from Berlin (`de-DE`) or Dubai (`en-AE`), our architecture ensures they land on a version of the site that feels intentional and reliable.

### Multi-Region Schema
Our `ProfessionalService` schema is dynamically expanded to include an `areaServed` property covering all major high-ticket markets. This tells Google's Knowledge Graph that our agency is not just a local shop in India, but a legitimate global entity.
