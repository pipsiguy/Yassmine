# Labubus: Mischief, Magic, and Toy Culture

Labubus are art toys created by Kasing Lung, known for their wild expressions, serrated teeth, and rabbit-like ears. They look a bit scary, but they're actually friendly and full of personality.

## Why are Labubus so popular?

- **Unique Look:** Labubus aren't your typical cute toys. Their "gross-cute" style stands out and appeals to adults and collectors.
- **Blind Box Fun:** You never know which Labubu you'll get, making collecting exciting and social.
- **Pop Mart Collabs:** Big brands like Pop Mart have made Labubus famous worldwide, with special editions and rare figures.

## The Community

Fans trade, collect, and share Labubus online. Some hunt for rare "chaser" figures, while others just love the playful designs.
