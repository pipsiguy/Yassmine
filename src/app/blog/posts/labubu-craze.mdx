## Who is Labubu?

Labubu is a distinctive character from "The Monsters" story series, created by the Hong Kong-born, Belgium-based artist **Kasing Lung**. With its serrated teeth, mischievous grin, and rabbit-like ears, Labubu might look a little scary at first glance, but it has a kind heart.

### The Rise of Art Toys

In recent years, "blind box" culture and designer toys have exploded in popularity. Pop Mart, a leading player in this industry, has collaborated with Kasing Lung to release numerous Labubu series, making the character accessible to a global audience.

### Why the Obsession?

1.  **Unique Aesthetic**: It's not your typical "cute" character. The "kimo-kawaii" (gross-cute) or edgy-cute aesthetic appeals to adults who want something different.
2.  **Collectibility**: The thrill of opening a blind box and finding a rare "chaser" or "secret" figure drives the community.
